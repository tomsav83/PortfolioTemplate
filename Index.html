ABOUT ME

Tom Savage

Having spent the majority of my career working in sales and claim handling roles, I returned to the UK in 2017 following a period travelling and working in Asia and Australia, looking for a career change.

With a keen interest in all things tech, I spoke to a careers advisor and decided to enroll on a Computer Science course at Trafford College.

After gaining a HND with Distinction in Digital Forensics and Cyber Security in 2019, I accepted the opportunity to further my qualification with an additional year of study at degree level, and graduated in 2020 with a First Class Honours degree in Network Security and Penetration Testing from Manchester Metropolitan University.

Searching for a role in the industry was difficult due to the pandemic but I eventually I landed a job working in account management for NCC Group with the intention of progressing through to a technical role when there was the opportunity. Unfortunatley, after speaking to my manager I was told that I would have to wait until summer 2022 until there would be a graduate technical role.

It was at this point that I re-evaluated my situation and reflected on what I enjoyed during my three years of studying. I covered modules in coding and really enjoyed the creative and problem solving nature of development and after speaking to friends in the industry and completing an online JS course I enrolled on 13 week masters full stack course to start my journey to become a developer.
